Evidence Room: Solve the Mysteries

Use the clues to write a logical deduction.

Clue 1: The suspect was in London during the robbery, but the robbery happened in Paris.

(Deduction: He / be / the thief)

Clue 2: Sarah's car is still in the driveway and her lights are on.

(Deduction: She / be / at home)

Clue 3: The window is broken and the jewelry is gone, but the alarm didn't go off.

(Deduction: The thief / know / the code)
