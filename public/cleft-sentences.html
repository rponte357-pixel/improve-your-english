Cleft Sentences

Mastering emphasis for advanced academic and formal discourse.

Why use them?

In C1 English, we use cleft sentences to focus on specific information. They "cut" a sentence in two to highlight the most important part.

Normal: I need a coffee.
It-Cleft: It is a coffee that I need.
What-Cleft: What I need is a coffee.
